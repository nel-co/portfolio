Disc Golf ScoreCard

The Overview

I designed the UI for a disc golf score keeping app. The goal was to create a clean and straight forward user interface that was easy to use. The competitors for this app all try to do to much, that's why with this app I focused on just the basic needs of the disc golfer. It allows users to add new courses/players, keep track of their score, and measure the distance of the throw.

The Process

My idea for the UI in regards to switching to the next hole is a swipeable card. The cards appear stacked on top of each other so that the user experiences a progression while playing through the round. When you're out on the course in the middle of the day, users should be able to just pull your phone out and enter the score. That's why I chose high contrasting colors so that it's easier see in the sunlight.
