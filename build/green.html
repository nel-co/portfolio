The Green Table

The Overview

The Green Table is a food and lifestyle blog. When approached to design the site, I was told it was to be primarily targeted to women between 25 and 60 years old. The blog content focuses on using organic, sustainable, and local ingredients sourced from farms and CSA sites. The goal while creating the site was to make it "modern but comfortable and homey."

The Process

After research and consulting with the client more, we knew we wanted to go with large images of the food for the main content.
